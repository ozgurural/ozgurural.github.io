FIRST-WAVE AGENTS · DELIVERY MILESTONES

Where the first agents earn their keep.

Four agents ship first because their value is largest and most measurable. Three run on shared central infrastructure for company-wide use; one deploys alongside simulators for live training-floor work. Each has a measurable ship date and a kill criterion, miss two milestones and it goes back to the drawing board.

  3. Wk 6Change-impact analysis and stale-doc detection run; suggested playbooks and corrective drafts generated for review.
